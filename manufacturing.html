Manufacturing Facilities

Coastal PEB has a top-notch 33,000 sft manufacturing facility in Autonagar, Guntur. For a task that requires quick and quality building, it’s essential to have a state-of-the-art facility. Ours has the latest machinery that comprises Pull Through Beam Welding Line, Sub Arc Welding Machine, Mig Machines, Rectifiers, Hydraulic & Mechanical Shearing Machines, Radial & Pillar Drilling Machines, EOT Cranes, C, Z, and Roof Sheet Roll Forming Machines so much so that Coastal PEB can deliver around 10000 MT of finished product per year.
